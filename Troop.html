Becoming a trooper with the SAHP

What it's like.

When joining SAHP, after your first time CSO training, you first will be ''shown around'' the Highway Patrol's offices and roads as a SAHP Cadet. when we find you are fit for the job you will be officially in service as a Trooper for the San Andreas Highway Patrol
