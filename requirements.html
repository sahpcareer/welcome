SAHP Requirements

Read this before applying

To make sure we only get the best of the best, there are some simple requirements for the SAHP to be able to apply for a position as trooper in the first place.

*Must have finished the full CSO training
*Must have finished the Exam with a 7 out of 10
*Must be atleast 14 years of age
*Must speak fluent English
*Must be prepared to work atleast 5 days a week
*Must be an American Citizen (or in process) -ingame
*Must be able to accept highly realistic processes
